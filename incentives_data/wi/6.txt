ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Barron Electric Cooperative offers a free Smart Electric Vehicle Charger ($1100.00 appox. value) to members who purchase an electric
vehicle and meet the requirements listed below. A $400 rebate is available to members who purchase an alternative charger that is
compatible with our load management equipment and meet the requirements listed below. Members are responsible for the costs
associated with the installation of the electric vehicle charger. If the charging station is not installed correctly, neither Barron Electric
nor the manufacturer will cover the cost of replacing or repairing the unit.
Smart Charger Requirements:
The charger works with all J1772 complying cars, as well as Tesla vehicles using Tesla's charging adapter.

      Equipment must be installed on cooperative's lines and may be inspected periodically.
      Members must participate in the load management program which interrupts charging from 2 p.m. - 9 p.m. ,daily.

          This Strategy is not interrupted on weekend or holidays, except during a full load control.
      Member usage data will be collected by software within the Smart Charger.
      The Smart Charger has a 3-year parts warranty. Please contact ZEF Energy or Clipper Creek for any warranty questions.
Alternative Charger Requirements:
      Equipment must be installed on cooperative's lines and may be inspected periodically.
      Members must participate in the load management program which interrupts charging from 2 p.m. - 9 p.m., daily.

          This Strategy is not interrupted on weekend or holidays, except during a full load control.
      Members must schedule an appointment with Barron Electric to install a radio receiver before receiving the rebate.
      The Rebate cannot exceed the cost of the electric car charger and a receipt must be provided.
      Tesla NEMA plug/adapter does not qualify
Business Members:
      Business members who want to install a charging station should contact the office to see if they are eligible.
  Please submit the following:
      Proof of vehicle purchase by member
      Copy of vehicle registration
      This completed form
      If Purchasing Alternative Charger - Copy of Receipt
