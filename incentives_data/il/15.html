Illinois Residential Instant Discounts and Rebates

You want to save on your energy bill – we want to help. Our Instant Discounts and Rebate offerings make it easier to upgrade your home equipment to energy-efficient models. Plus, you can save even more with tax credits and rebates available through the Department of Energy’s (DOE) Energy Savings Hub.
 

Instant Discounts

Save money right away when you purchase equipment from qualified dealers and installers!
 
Available for qualifying models of:
  • Central air conditioners
  • Natural gas furnaces
 
Find a provider

Rebates

Get money back after your new equipment is installed!
 
Available for qualifying models of:
  • Air-source heat pumps
  • Ductless mini-splits
  • Smart thermostats
 
Apply now
 

Instant Discount Offerings

Our Instant Discounts program incentivizes local distributors to stock energy-efficient equipment, making it easier for you to find more energy-saving models in your area and reduce your energy bills and overall carbon footprint. Ask your preferred HVAC contractor if they participate in MidAmerican Energy Instant Discounts.
 
Equipment Discount Qualifications
Central air conditioners $101 – $412/unit 🗎
Natural gas furnaces $60 – $213/unit 🗎
 
Find a provider
 

Rebate Offerings

Get money back after you’ve purchased and installed your new energy-saving equipment. 
 
Equipment Rebate Qualifications
Air-source heat pumps $400 – $600/unit 🗎
Ductless mini-splits $150 – $600/unit 🗎
Smart thermostats Up to $75/unit 🗎
 
Apply now
 
 

Keep in mind

  • Review the Illinois Qualifications and Conditions to make sure you qualify.
  • If installing heating or cooling equipment, ensure the equipment is AHRI Certified®.
  • If installing a smart thermostat, ensure the thermostat is ENERGY STAR® certified.
  • Instant Discounts are only available by purchasing your equipment through a participating Instant Discounts provider.
  • Instant Discounts cannot be combined with any other rebate or incentive program, including prescriptive rebates.
  • When applying for a rebate online, have all necessary documents for upload, including an itemized sales receipt or invoice.
  • Check the date – be sure to apply for rebates within 90 days of purchase or by December 31, 2023, whichever comes first.
 
Questions? For more information or to request a paper rebate application, email energyexpert@midamerican.com or call 877-932-0616.
