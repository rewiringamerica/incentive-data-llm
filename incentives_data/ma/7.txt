Danvers Electric Division
Heat Pump (Ducted or Ductless mini-split) and Central AC
DED is pleased to offer rebates for qualified ducted and ductless (mini-split) heat pumps and central A/C systems . Please read the terms and conditions below to ensure that you qualify for a rebate.

Eligibility Requirements & Restrictions:

 Your electric account must be active, residential, and in good standing. Applications from accounts not in good standing will be held until full payment is received. A DPW representative will contact you for payment.
 The heat pump rebate is calculated at $1,000 per ton. 1 Ton = 12,000 BTU/hr, measured a 95캟.
 Applications require a copy of your installer invoice, which must include an itemized list of units installed, model numbers, SEER, EER & HSPF ratings,and AHRI certificate
 Incomplete applications may delay processing by 4-6 weeks. Normal processing time for complete applications is 8-10 weeks. Appliance must be installed at service address location.
 Applications must be submitted to the application portal or postmarked within 365 days of purchase
 Offer is subject to funding and can expire at any time. DED can also terminate the program at any time.

 Equipment must meet the below requirements to qualify for the rebate:
Ducted Heat Pump: SEER 16+ / HSPF 9.5+
Ductless Heat Pump (mini split): SSER 18+ / HSPF 10+
Central AC:
EER 13+ / SEER16.5+
 Danvers Electric Division does not warrant, endorse or assume liability for the quality, performance, or safety of the contractor, retailer and/or wholesaler or performance of any appliance or fixture. Responsibility for choosing the product is solely the applicant's. Licensed, insured, qualified installations only. The town of Danvers assumes no liability for installation.
 You agree to allow DED to inspect appliance installation on your premises. Applicant agrees and understands that the Danvers Electric Division does not guarantee that the installation of an air-source heat pump or central AC will result in energy savings, as site-specific energy consumption varies.

Please note that your rebate amount is determined by the incentive program in effect on the date of your purchase and may differ from the current offering. If you have any questions about the rebate your appliance qualifies for, please contact the Energy Resource Manager
before submitting your application.
