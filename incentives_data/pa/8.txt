ENERGY STAR® Certified Clothes Dryer
Up to $50 Rebate (+$25 for Income-Eligible Customers)
ENERGY STAR certified clothes dryers deliver superior efficiency and performance by incorporating advanced features—while using 20% less energy than standard models. ENERGY STAR certified products are widely available at local and online retailers.
￼
 Look for the ENERGY STAR label to find qualified products.
￼

Which Clothes Dryers Qualify?
To be eligible for the $25 rebate, the dryer must be ENERGY STAR certified. To be eligible for the $50 rebate, the dryer must be certified as ENERGY STAR Most Efficient.
 
See application for all eligibility requirements. Rebates are available to residential customers of FirstEnergy's Pennsylvania electric companies on qualified products and services purchased between June 1, 2021, and May 31, 2026. Rebate applications must be submitted within 90 days of purchase.
How to receive your rebate:
 
￼
Apply online
Submit your rebate application online with proof of purchase. This option is available for qualifying products purchased online or in-stores. Additional terms and conditions may apply. See rebate forms for details.
￼
Unable to purchase in-store or apply online?
You can download a rebate application, then submit it with proof of purchase, by email at FirstEnergyRebates@franklinenergy.com or by mail to FirstEnergy's PA electric companies Residential Rebates Program, PO Box 510861, New Berlin, WI 53151.
￼
Wait! You may be eligible for an additional $25 rebate.
Income-eligible customers can apply for an additional $25 rebate for qualifying purchases.

For purchases of qualified products that require a rebate application, use the Apply Online or Download Application links above and select "Yes" to the question "Do you believe you may be qualified for income-eligible programs?" on the rebate form. If you already submitted your rebate application and forgot to check that you are income-eligible, click here to download and complete this application.
