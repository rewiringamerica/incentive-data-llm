ENERGY STAR® Certified Ductless Mini-Split HP
A $200 Rebate
This product can cut heating costs in half compared to conventional electric heating systems. Because they transfer instead of generating heat, ENERGY STAR certified ductless mini-split heat pumps use 60% less energy than standard home electric resistance-based heating systems. Please work with your contractor to complete and submit your rebate application.
￼
 Look for the ENERGY STAR label to find qualified products.
￼

Qualifications: 
 
	•	Must be ENERGY STAR certified.
	•	HVAC system must be rated in the AHRI database.
	•	Mini-split heat pump systems must be certified by AHRI as having the below minimum SEER2, EER2 and HSPF2 requirements:
￼
	•	AHRI certificate must include all installed components, as stated on invoice. Values stated on AHRI certificate must meet minimum program qualifications. For example, a condenser may have an “up to 15 SEER” rating, but when matched with a compressor and furnace, the system may only reach 14.5 SEER as determined by AHRI. Ask your contractor for more details.  
	•	AHRI ratings for individual components of a system will not be considered valid.
	•	Rebate available for conditioning of domestic living space only. 
	•	Ducted Mini-Split systems qualify under Air-Source Heat Pumps.
￼

Which Ductless Heat Pumps Qualify?
To be eligible for the $200 rebate, the ductless mini-split HP must be ENERGY STAR certified. 
 
See application for all eligibility requirements. Rebates are available to residential customers of FirstEnergy's Pennsylvania electric companies on qualified products and services purchased between June 1, 2021, and May 31, 2026. Rebate applications must be submitted within 90 days of purchase.
How to receive your rebate:
