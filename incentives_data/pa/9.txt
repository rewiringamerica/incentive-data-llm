ENERGY STAR® Certified Smart Thermostat
A $50 Rebate (+$25 for Income-Eligible Customers)
ENERGY STAR certified smart thermostats can save an average of 8% on heating and cooling costs.
￼
 Look for the ENERGY STAR label to find qualified products.
￼

Which Smart Thermostats Qualify?
To be eligible for a $50 rebate, the smart thermostat must be ENERGY STAR certified. 
 
See application for all eligibility requirements. Rebates are available to residential customers of FirstEnergy's Pennsylvania electric companies on qualified products and services purchased between June 1, 2021, and May 31, 2026. Rebate applications must be submitted within 90 days of purchase.
Two ways to receive your rebate:
￼
Save instantly at checkout!
You can receive your rebate as an in-store discount at the time of purchase—no rebate form required! This is available for select, qualifying products that are purchased in-store or online at participating Lowe's stores inside the service territory of FirstEnergy’s Pennsylvania electric companies.
￼
￼
Apply online
Submit your rebate application online with proof of purchase. This option is available for qualifying products purchased online or in-stores. Additional terms and conditions may apply. See rebate forms for details.
￼
Unable to purchase in-store or apply online?
You can download a rebate application, then submit it with proof of purchase, by email at FirstEnergyRebates@franklinenergy.com or by mail to FirstEnergy's PA electric companies Residential Rebates Program, PO Box 510861, New Berlin, WI 53151.
￼
Wait! You may be eligible for an additional $25 rebate.
Income-eligible customers can apply for an additional $25 rebate for qualifying purchases one of two ways:

1
If you received an instant discount at Lowe’s or The Home Depot, download and complete this application to apply for your income-eligible rebate.

2
For purchases of qualified products that require a rebate application, use the Apply Online or Download Application links above and select "Yes" to the question "Do you believe you may be qualified for income-eligible programs?" on the rebate form. Click here to download and complete this application.
