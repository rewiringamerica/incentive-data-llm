Electric Heat Pump Clothes Dryer
Receive a $300 rebate when you purchase a new, high-efficiency electric heat pump clothes dryer. Receive an additional $20 back if you purchase your electric heat pump clothes dryer in Pasadena.
￼
Electric heat pump clothes dryers are one of the most energy efficient options available on the market because it uses a sophisticated heat exchange system to conserve and recycle the same air. Heat pump technology moves heat instead of creating heat by burning natural gas, so it consumes less energy than a standard natural gas clothes dryer. Since its drying air temperature is considerably lower, electric heat pump clothes dryers are also less harmful on your garments. Electric Heat pump clothes dryers also do not require ventilation set-up, since it doesn’t create heat or moisture in the surrounding room. More importantly, all-electric appliances like the electric heat pump clothes dryer produces less carbon emissions than its natural gas counterparts since it doesn’t require the combustion of fossil fuels during operation, thereby improving indoor air quality and the health of our environment.
How To Qualify
	•	Purchase and install a new, high-efficiency electric heat pump clothes dryer that is ENERGY STAR ® certified
	•	Purchase your new electric heat pump clothes dryer in Pasadena for an additional $20 rebate
	•	Submit your online rebate application after installs are completed, along with a copy of your detailed receipt/invoice and the required supporting documents, within 180 days from date of purchase.
How to Apply
	•	Log in to your PWP account
	•	Select “Apply for Rebate”
	•	Please upload your complete sales receipt or paid invoice, including:
	◦	Product and model number
	◦	Date purchased and delivered
	◦	Appliance Specification Sheet
	◦	Supporting documents for ENERGY STAR® rating
	▪	ENERGY STAR® certificate or EnergyGuide label with ENERGY STAR® logo
	•	Once approved, your rebate check will arrive in 6 to 8 weeks
Terms
	•	Only residential PWP electric customers are eligible
	•	Limit one per customer, every 10 years
Disclaimer
This program is subject to verification of the purchase and installation of the product(s) before and/or after the rebate has been paid. Completed applications must be received within 180 days from the date of purchase to qualify. Installs must be completed before submitting your rebate application. PWP reserves the right to modify the terms of this program at any time. Rebate amounts will be calculated according to the program incentives at the date of application submission. Program funds are limited and available to eligible PWP customers. Rebates apply to new appliance and product purchases only (refurbished/reconditioned do not qualify). Customers must be enrolled in one of PWP's income-qualified bill payment assistance programs at the time of application submission in order to qualify for the bonus.
