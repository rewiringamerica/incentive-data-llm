Product Eligibility  Equipment purchased more than 90 days prior to application or date of contractor invoice is not eligible.  Must comply with District electric service requirements.  Only new main panels qualify for incentive. Must be a minimum of 200 amps.  Upgrade must include dedicated circuit for at least (2) of the following: o EV Charger o Heat Pump Water Heater o Heat Pump (HVAC) o Solar PV  Upgrade must be done for purposes of electrification.  Upgrade must have at least one of the listed electrification measures installed at time of rebate.  Project must be permitted.  Upgrade must be completed within 90 days of application.
Program Terms  Applicant must be a current electric customer with an active TDPUD account to be eligible for electric rebates.  Application must include dated sales receipt(s), manufacturer/model number of eligible product(s) or service(s) clearly identified, including contractor's name.  Rebates are issued to the account holder in the form of a one-time check payment. Payments are typically mailed within 6-8 weeks of TDPUD receiving the application.  If account holder is a landlord and account is inactive at the time of rebate issuance, a check will be mailed to the landlord address on file.  Rebate not to exceed the cost of equipment.  Photo of old panel, prior to start of upgrade is required.  Photo(s) of new installed panel must be provided.  Photo/copy of permits must be provided.  Application must be signed, dated and contain all supporting documentation.
