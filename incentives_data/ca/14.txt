Lodi Insulation Rebate
Offered to Lodi Electric Utility residential single-family and multi-family properties for the purchase and installation of qualified new Insulation upgrades.
This application should be submitted within six (6) months of the purchase or installation date.
Rebates are limited to 50% of the project’s total cost.
To qualify for the insulation rebate, the home must be equipped with Central Air Conditioning, Heat Pump, and/or Electric Heat.
Rebate Amounts
Attic insulation: $0.75 per sq. ft.
	•	Existing Attic condition: R-19 or lower
	•	Final Attic condition: R-30 or higher
Wall insulation: $0.30 per sq. ft.
	•	Existing Wall condition: R-0
	•	Final Wall condition: R-13 or higher
Rebates under $300 are paid as a credit on the customer’s utility account. For rebates over $300, the customer will be sent a check.
Please note: Your project’s final invoice(s)/receipt(s) are required to submit this application.

Allow 12 to 16 weeks to receive the rebate, if all qualifications are met.
