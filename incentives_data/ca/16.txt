Lodi Air Source Heat Pump Rebate
Offered to Lodi Electric Utility residential customers for purchase and installation of a new qualifying Air Source Heat Pump.
This application should be submitted within six (6) months of the purchase or installation date.
Rebates are limited to 50% of the project’s total cost.
Rebate Amounts
Split or Unitary/Package Ducted Systems:
$150 per ton *Minimum 16.0 SEER and 9.0 HSPF (equivalent to 15.3 SEER2 and 7.7 HSPF2) $200 per ton *Minimum 17.0 SEER and 10.0 HSPF (equivalent to 16.2 SEER2 and 8.5 HSPF2). $250 per ton *Minimum 18.0 SEER and 11.0 HSPF (equivalent to 17.2 SEER2 and 9.4 HSPF2).
Mini/Multi-Split Ductless Systems:
$250 per ton *Minimum 16.0 SEER and 9.0 HSPF (equivalent to 15.3 SEER2 and 7.7 HSPF2)
*Equipment must be new and certified by the Air Conditioning, Heating, and Refrigeration Institute (AHRI). Equipment tonnage is calculated using the cooling capacity listed on the AHRI certificate. Divide cooling capacity by 12,000 (1 ton = 12,000 Btuh), the result is not rounded. Example: AHRI cooling capacity 34,500/12,000 = 2.87-ton unit.
Rebates under $300 are paid as a credit on the customer’s utility account. For rebates over $300, the customer will be sent a check.

Allow 12 to 16 weeks to receive the rebate, if all qualifications are met.
