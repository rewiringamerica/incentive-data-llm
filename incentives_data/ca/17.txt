Lodi Whole House Fan Rebate
Offered to Lodi Electric Utility residential customers for purchase and installation of a new, Whole-House Fan with Insulated Backdraft Dampers.
This application should be submitted within six (6) months of the purchase or installation date.
Rebates are limited to 50% of the project’s total cost.
Before you get started
	•	Qualifying single-family homes must have central air conditioning or heat pumps without a whole-house fan or other automated fresh air sources.
	•	New whole-house fans must be permanently installed in the upper floor ceiling of the conditioned space of the home and must have insulated backdraft dampers.
	•	Garage whole-house fans are not eligible for rebates.
	•	Your final invoice/receipt is required to submit this application.
Rebate: $300
Customer will be sent a check.

Allow 12 to 16 weeks to receive the rebate, if all qualifications are met.
