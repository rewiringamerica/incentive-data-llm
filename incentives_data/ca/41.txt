Home Appliance Rebates
You rely on your appliances to keep your household running smoothly. Today's energy- and water- efficient home appliances and fixtures do everything you need while using less energy and less water, which helps you save money.  Replace your home appliances and fixtures with efficient models and get a rebate from PWP.
PWP rebates are available on Refrigerator, Dishwasher, Ceiling Fan and Room Air Conditioner EnergyStar products, to learn more about how the EPA defines Energy Star, visit the Energy Star website.
