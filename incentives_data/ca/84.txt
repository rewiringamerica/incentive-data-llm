Equipment Eligibility
 Equipment purchased more than 90 days prior to application is not eligible.  Equipment must be new purchase and not used.  Appliances must be EnergyStar certified.  Induction Cooktop must be 30" or larger. Both Standalone and ranges with built-in induction cooktops are eligible.
Program Terms & Conditions
 Applicant must be a current electric customer with an active TDPUD account to be eligible for electric rebates.  Application must include dated sales receipt, eligible equipment or service(s) clearly identified.  Rebates are issued to the account holder as a one-time credit to active TDPUD account. Credits are typically applied within 6-8 weeks of
TDPUD receiving the application.  If account holder is a landlord and account is inactive at the time of rebate issuance, a check will be mailed to the address on file.  Rebates are limited to (1) rebate per appliance type per account every 10 years.  Rebate not to exceed the cost of equipment.  Customer agrees to allow verification of make/model, efficiency & installation by an authorized District representative if requested.  "Before" photo(s) of original cooktop or range in place including nameplate and gas hook-ups must be submitted with rebate application.  "After" photo(s) of new installed cooktop or range including nameplate must be submitted with rebate application.  Rebate application must be signed and dated.
