Lodi ENERGY STAR® Smart Thermostat Rebate
Offered to Lodi Electric Utility residential customers for purchase and installation of a new, ENERGY STAR® certified Smart Thermostat.
This application should be submitted within six (6) months of the purchase or installation date.
Rebates are limited to 50% of the cost (limit two devices per home).
Limit of one thermostat rebate per household.
Before you get started
	•	New device must have ENERGY STAR® certification.
	•	Smart Thermostat must control Central Air Conditioner, Heat Pump, or Electric Forced Air Furnace.
	•	Your final invoice/receipt is required to submit this application.
Rebate: $50
Paid as a credit on customer’s utility account.

Allow 12 to 16 weeks to receive the rebate if all qualifications are met.
