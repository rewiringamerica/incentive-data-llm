Ceiling Insulation Rebate
Upgrade your ceiling insulation and receive $0.10 per square foot. Get an extra $0.05 per square foot back when you use a qualified local contractor or purchase in Pasadena.
￼


 
How to Qualify
	•	Insulation must be added to the attic or crawl space above your ceiling, or to the roof of a non-vented attic
	•	The new insulation must increase your ceiling’s R-value to R-30 or higher
	•	Hire a qualified local contractor or purchase in Pasadena and receive an additional $0.05 back per sq. ft.
	•	Submit your online rebate application after installs are completed, along with a copy of your detailed receipt/invoice and the required supporting documents, within 180 days from date of purchase.
How to Apply
	•	Log in to your PWP account
	•	Select “Apply for Rebate”
	•	Please upload electronic copies of the following documents:
	◦	Your complete sales receipt or paid invoice, including:
	▪	Proof of square footage installed
	▪	Product and model number
	▪	Proof of final R-value
	▪	Date purchased and installed
	▪	Product specification sheet
	•	Once approved, your rebate check will arrive in 6 to 8 weeks
Terms
	•	Only residential PWP electric utility customers are eligible.
	•	Retrofit projects that require an insulation upgrade to meet Title 24 requirements are not eligible.
	•	Limit 1 rebate per property, every 15 years.
	•	Home Improvement Program insulation recipients are not eligible.
	•	New construction does NOT qualify
	•	Are you enrolled in PWP's income-qualified Bill Payment Assistance program? If so, you can receive a $0.10 bonus per square foot of insulation installed. Visit the Under One Roof Program webpage to learn more.
	•	Additional Rebates Available: Additional incentives may also be available from the Southern California (SoCal) Gas Company. For information on the SoCal Gas rebate, visit their Available Rebates page
Disclaimer
This program is subject to verification of the purchase and installation of the product(s) before and/or after the rebate has been paid. Completed applications must be received within 180 days from the date of purchase to qualify. Installs must be completed before submitting your rebate application. PWP reserves the right to modify the terms of this program at any time. Rebate amounts will be calculated according to the program incentives at the date of application submission. Program funds are limited and available to eligible PWP customers. Rebates apply to new appliance and product purchases only (refurbished/reconditioned do not qualify). Customers must be enrolled in one of PWP's income-qualified bill payment assistance programs at the time of application submission in order to qualify for the bonus.
