Lodi ENERGY STAR® Hybrid/Heat Pump Water Heater Rebate
Offered to Lodi Electric Utility residential customers for purchase and installation of a new ENERGY STAR® certified Hybrid/Heat Pump Water Heater.
This application should be submitted within six (6) months of the purchase or installation date.
Rebates are limited to 50% of the total project cost.
Before you get started
	•	Eligible homes must have an electric water heater.
	•	Homes with existing gas water heaters do not qualify for this rebate.
	•	New Hybrid-Heat Pump Water Heater must have ENERGY STAR® certification.
	•	40-80 gallon models are eligible for the rebate (40-gallon units do not qualify).
	•	Your final invoice/receipt is required to submit this application.
Rebate: $200
Paid as a credit on customer’s utility account.

Allow 12 to 16 weeks to receive the rebate if all qualifications are met.
