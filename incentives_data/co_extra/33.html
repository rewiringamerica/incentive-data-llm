Solar PV Rebates

There are solar and storage rebates available for Eagle County residents. See below for options and contact the Walking Mountains Energy & Buildings Team with questions!

Green Financing Options

Walking Mountains Science Center (Greater Eagle County: Vail to Dotsero)

Solar PV- unincorporated Eagle County (not including Roaring Fork Valley, visit CORE for RFV rebates)

Available for installation of renewable generation for net metering at premises

  • Must be approved for a Holy Cross Energy Renewables Rebate or Xcel Energy’s Solar Rewards
  • The following incentives are available:
    • 0-6kW-$250/kW
    • 6-25 kW-$100/kW

Contact the Team

Rebate funding is limited and the availability is subject to end at any time. A rebate can be confirmed/reserved with a signed Solarize contract.

Holy Cross Energy Rebates

Solar PV rebate:

0-6 kW = $250/kW
6-25 kW = $100/kW

Battery Storage rebate:

0-25 kW = $250/kW, if not using Power+
Learn more about the Power+ Program for battery storage here.

*Must meet requirements, contact Holy Cross Energy for details.

Learn More About HCE’s Renewable Energy and Battery Incentives

Location Specific Rebates

Towns of Avon, Eagle, and Vail (including Town of Vail employees) 

  • $1,000 per property

Unincorporated Eagle County (not including Roaring Fork Valley, visit CORE for RFV rebates)

  • 0-6kW- $250/kW
  • 6-25kW- $100/kW
