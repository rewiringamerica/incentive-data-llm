Tax Rebates on Solar Installations
Boulder residents and businesses that have installed solar electric or solar thermal (hot water) systems on their property may be eligible for a sales and use tax rebate.

About
The City of Boulder's Solar Rebate Ordinance was enacted in 2008 and may rebate approximately 15 percent of the city sales and use tax paid on materials and permits for the solar installation.

Eligibility
To be eligible, taxpayers must file a rebate application within 12 months of the city's final inspection.
