Stay efficient—and comfortable—with an insulation upgrade
Drafts and thin insulation don’t just make things uncomfortable—they actually cost you money! From gaps in your doors and doorframes to poorly rated insulation in your walls and attic, shoring up your home’s weather stripping and insulation helps keep things more comfortable and makes your heating and cooling more efficient and cost-effective.
Thankfully, there are rebates available for upgrading your home insulation. And federal tax credits are  also available for insulation improvements. Learn more in the Inflation Reduction Act linked below.
Eligibility and details
If you heat with electricity and work with a contractor to upgrade your insulation, you may quality for a rebate of 20 percent off specific applications up to $1,400.
	•	Wall insulation—receive up to $500 in rebates.
	•	Attic insulation—receive up to $400 in rebates.
	•	Air sealing—receive up to $400 in rebates.
To qualify:
	•	This program is available only to Minnesota residential customers only.
	•	Your home’s primary heat must be electric.
	•	You have not previously received an insulation rebate for your home this year.
	•	Your home is not new construction.
	•	Your projects meet specific R-factor levels:
	◦	Homes with attic insulation levels at or below R-20 must bring their total level to R-44.
	◦	Homes with wall insulation levels at or below R-8 must bring their level to R-13.
Apply for your rebate
For questions about program eligibility or rebates, contact our Idea Center via email, or call 800-493-3299 during business hours.
To apply for a rebate you may apply online, or fill out the form below and attach all required documentation, then email us or send to:
