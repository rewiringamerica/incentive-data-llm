Eligibility and details
Minnesota customers will receive $50 for each qualifying standard-sized ENERGY-STAR®-rated appliance. Eligible appliances include:

	•	Clothes washers
	•	Electric clothes dryers
Limit one rebate per appliance type. Proof of purchase must be included. Compact refrigerators and freezers (less than 7.5 cubic feet) do not qualify.
