Line Voltage Programmable Thermostat Rebate

$20 Rebate on qualifying* Programmable Thermostats

*Non-internet connected thermostat enables pre-set heating schedules. Only controls electric heat sources, most commonly baseboard heaters.

Rebate Form | Participating Retailers | Participating Contractors | Participating Utilities

APPLY ONLINE!
Click here to apply for your rebate through our easy online form!


Available on purchases made January 1–December 31, 2023


How to Get Your Rebate on a Line Voltage Programmable Thermostat


  1. Purchase a qualifying line voltage programmable thermostat.
    Note: You must be a residential retail customer of a participating utility to be eligible. Qualifying equipment does not include reconditioned or resale equipment.
  2. Submit your online rebate request here, or submit a completed paper rebate form (download here or pick up at participating retailer). Include a copy of the sales receipt to the address on the rebate form. Expect your rebate check in 8–10 weeks.

OR

  1. Work with a participating HVAC contractor.
  2. Contractor will install your new programmable thermostat and submit the rebate for you.

For questions about the status of your rebate, call 1-855-669-8196.
