Rebates are available for several electric heating technologies installed on a qualified off-peak rate when you install a minimum of 9 kilowatts (kW).

Rebate form

Electric heating rebates

Install an electric plenum heater, electric boiler, or electric furnace OR electric cable or panel storage heating on one on our Dual Fuel or RDC Rate to qualify for the following rebates.  

  • $500 for installing a 9 kW to 5 kW system.
  • $20 per kW for systems larger than 25 kW.

A plenum heater sits within your duct work just above your furnace to create a dual fuel system.

An electric boiler heats liquid for radiator, baseboard, and hydronic floor heating systems.

An electric furnace is a whole-house electric resistance heating system.

Electric cable or panel storage heating is installed under the slab during new construction.

Additional rebates are available for baseboard, cove heat, radiant ceiling, or non-storage floor-warming heat installed beneath flooring material.*

  • Receive a $15 per kW.

All offer 100 percent efficient electric heating! 

Heat storage systems

Get a rebate of $40 per kilowatt for qualified systems up to 500 kilowatts in size when you install a minimum of 9 kW on our Deferred Load, RDC, or Fixed Time of Delivery Rate. A typical home system is about 28 kW and would qualify for a rebate of about $1,200!

Eligible electric applications include:

  • Central storage furnaces
  • Thermal storage room units
  • Underfloor cable or panel systems
  • Electric boilers installed to serve underfloor hydronic storage systems
  • Ice storage applications for commercial space cooling

Heat pump rebates

Additional generous rebates are available for heat pump installations when you install a qualified cold-climate, air-to-water, or geothermal heat pump. Rebates vary by state and range from $400 to $1,200 per ton of installed capacity.

Electric water heating

Electric water heaters operate at up to 96 percent efficiency and qualify for the following rebates when installed on an off-peak service. 

  • Get a $300 rebate when you install an 80-gallon or larger water heater new to off-peak control.
  • Get a $150 rebate for a replacement.
  • Get a $150 rebate when you install a 50-gallon water heater new to off-peak control.
  • Get a $75 rebate for a replacement unit.
  • Get a $40 per kW rebate for installing a heavy duty commercial water heating system when you install at least 12 kW and 120-gallons of storage. A $5,000 maximum rebate applies.

Water heaters must be rated with a minimum .90 UEF (uniform energy factor) to qualify.

You’ll save every month with one of our a discounted off-peak rates. Install your water heater on our Dual Fuel, Deferred Load, RDC, or Controlled Water Heating Rates and take advantage of a lower rate. Or simply let us install a radio receiver to your residential or general service meter and receive a monthly bill credit of $8 to $10 (varies by state).
