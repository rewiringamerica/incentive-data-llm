Get better efficiency—and get $50!

Upgrading your appliances to ENERGY STAR®-rated appliances gives you better efficiency and energy savings. It’ll also get you a quick $50 back for each qualified appliance!

Eligibility and details

Minnesota customers will receive $50 for each qualifying standard-sized ENERGY-STAR®-rated appliance. Eligible appliances include:

  • Refrigerators
  • Chests or upright freezers
  • Dishwashers
  • Clothes washers
  • Electric clothes dryers

Limit one rebate per appliance type. Proof of purchase must be included. Compact refrigerators and freezers (less than 7.5 cubic feet) do not qualify.

Apply for your rebate

Fill out the rebate form and attach all required documentation. Please allow 6-8 weeks for your rebate check to arrive.
