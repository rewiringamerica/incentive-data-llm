2023 Smart Thermostats Rebate

Smart Thermostats Rebate 
	•	$25 per thermostat or $50 per thermostat if account on EEA Time Of Use Demand Rate
	•	Limit 2 thermostats per member (members are allowed up to 5 line voltage Smart thermostats).
	•	Thermostat must be installed at the consumer account location.
A line voltage smart thermostat regulates heating and cooling systems and operates on line voltage of of 240V or 120V. In addition, smart systems can stay connected through WiFi connectivity and compatible mobile apps. If your home uses electric baseboard heat or in-ceiling electric resistance, a line voltage smart thermostat might be required.
