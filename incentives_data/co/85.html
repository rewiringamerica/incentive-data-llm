Rebate & Recycling Programs

  • Appliance Rebate
  • Refrigerator Recycling Program

Replacing your old appliances with newer, more energy-efficient products is a quick way to see immediate savings on your monthly statement. To help you make the most of your energy, United Power members can now receive rebates on the purchase of NEW Energy Star and select efficient electric appliances. United Power will also provide a recycling credit when you have your old refrigerator or freezer recycled.

For a list of qualified appliances, go to EnergyStar.gov. More details can be found in the accordion sections below. For more information, contact United Powers Energy Management team at 303-637-1311.

Appliance Rebate Details

Available Rebates

  • New Energy Star Clothes Dryer (standard electric) $30
  • New Energy Star Clothes Dryer (heat pump) $90
  • Induction cooktop (30" or larger - gas-to-electric conversion) $350

Program Details

  • You must receive permanent electric service from United Power to qualify.
  • Applications must be submitted within 90 days of purchase.
  • Rebates in this category will be issued as a bill credit. Allow 6to 8 weeks for rebate processing.
  • United Power reserves the right to inspect the installation of any rebated appliance.
  • Refrigerator/Freezer Rebates for full-size refrigerators only (7.75 cu. ft. minimum).
  • Replacement of gas range with induction requires verification of old gas cooktop/range. Photographs of the old unit are sufficient.

Rebate Application Checklist

  • Rebate application
  • Copy of purchase receipt
  • Original yellow Energy Guide label
  • Proof of installation (induction cooktops only)

What is Energy Star?

Look for this logo on your new appliance purchases. TheEnergy Starlogo is on all qualified products that meet specific standards for energy efficiency. Qualified products exceed federal minimum standards for efficiency and quality sometimes significantly. Look for the label on appliances, electronics, water heaters, windows and other products that consumeenergy in your home.

About Energy Star

EnergyGuide Label

To help you figure out whether an appliance is energy efficient, the federal government requires most appliances to display the bright yellow and black EnergyGuide label. Although these labels will now show you which appliance is the most efficient on the market, they will show you the annual energy consumption and operating cost for each appliance so you can compare them yourself.

Using the EnergyGuide Label

Refrigerator/Freezer Recycling Credit

When an old refrigerator is moved to a garage or basement, it takes twice as much energy to keep your food cold and can cost more than $100 a year to operate. To encourage recycling of old refrigerators, United Power members qualify for a refrigerator/freezer recycling credit to properly dispose of their old refrigerator or freezer.

Recycling Credit $60

Program Details

  • You must receive permanent electric service from United Power to qualify.
  • Applications must be submitted within 90 days of purchase.
  • Rebates in this category will be issued as a bill credit. Allow 6 to 8 weeks for rebate processing.
  • United Power reserves the right to inspect the installation of any rebated appliance.
  • Refrigerator/Freezer Recycling Credits for full-size refrigerators only (7.75 cu. ft. minimum).
  • Members are limited to two (2) recycling credits per membership per year.
  • Proof of recycling must be submitted with the application. Qualified proofof recycling includesverification of retailer haul away (indicated on sales or delivery receipt) or receipt from an appliance recycling service that reclaims the refrigerant.

Recycling Credit Application Checklist

  • Rebate application
  • Proof of retailer haul away and/or recycling receipt

Flip Your Fridge

Older refrigerators are costly to operate and leave a largecarbon footprint on the environment. In fact, refrigerator models 15 years or older use twice as much energy as new Energy Star models. Why not recycle that old energy wasting fridge, and replace it with a new Energy Star certified model? You will save money and help the environment.Find the exact savings with theEnergy Star Savings Calculator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you need the original EnergyGuide label from my appliance?

The ORIGINAL EnergyGuide label is required for all rebate requests in order to verify purchase of an approved Energy Star appliance. Your model number should appear in the upper right corner of your EnergyGuide label.

I didnt buy a new refrigerator, but I want to recycle the one in my garage. Can I get a Recycling Credit?

Yes. United Powers Recycling Credit is available to members for recycling old refrigerators. You must provide proof that either a retailer hauled your old appliance away or that a reputable appliance recycler disposed of your refrigerator or freezer.

Recycling credits are limited to two (2) per membership, per year.
