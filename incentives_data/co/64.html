Rebates for All Your Heating & Cooling Needs

Heating and cooling your home and water are some of the largest household energy expenses – accounting for up to 60% of total energy use!

Central Air Conditioner (Ducted) – forgo traditional ducts or air passages to control the temperatures for separate zones. Great for adding AC or for home expansions.

Evaporative Cooler – uses the evaporation of water via integrated water media pad(s) and a fan to cool your building.

Heat Pumps – the most advanced and efficient heating and cooling system available, all in one product! Energy experts recommend heat pumps as the most economical way to keep your home or business comfortable year-round. Both ducted and ductless versions are available to meet your unique needs.

Thermostats – we offer rebates for both smart thermostats and line voltage thermostats. Participate in our Power Peak Rewards program to get rewards.

Water Heaters – various electric options can provide sufficient hot water for your family while being energy efficient, saving on the cost of energy needed to heat your water.

Whole House Fans – a type of fan used for cooling, these are designed to circulate air within your home. They pull air in from open windows and exhausts it through the attic and roof.
